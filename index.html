What is a credit and when is it deducted?+
One credit = one successfully scraped Maps listing. Credits are only deducted for successfully scraped results — if a listing fails, no credit is spent.
Do credits expire?+
No — credits never expire. Your 25 free starter credits and any purchased packs (Pro 500 credits / Enterprise 2,500 credits) stay in your account forever. Use them at your own pace.
Which browsers are supported?+
The extension works on all Chromium-based browsers: Chrome, Edge, Brave and Opera. Firefox support is in development.
How accurate is the email finder?+
The scraper visits each business website and contact/about pages, extracting emails via regex. Typical hit rates are 40–65% depending on niche and city.
