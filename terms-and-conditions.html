3 Description of Service

Maps Lead Scraper is a Chrome-compatible browser extension (Manifest V3) that:

  • Scrapes publicly listed business information from Google Maps search results
  • Extracts 8 data fields per listing: Business Name, Email Address, Star Rating, Opening Hours, Website URL, Phone Number, Full Address, and Category
  • Visits business websites to automatically find email addresses via pattern matching
  • Exports results as a CSV file with auto-download to your Downloads folder
  • Provides a built-in results viewer with search, filter, sort, and one-click Open in Google Sheets / Excel Online

The Extension works on Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Opera browsers. Firefox support is not currently available.
